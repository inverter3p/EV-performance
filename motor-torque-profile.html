Motion Drive Profile Plotter

Compare rotary motor-drive dynamics and its linear-motion companion equation from an input motion profile. The app estimates acceleration numerically, then calculates required effort and mechanical power.

$$ T_e(t)=T_L(t)+J\frac{d\omega(t)}{dt}+B_r\omega(t), \qquad P_m(t)=T_e(t)\omega(t) $$ $$ F_e(t)=F_L(t)+m\frac{dv(t)}{dt}+B_l v(t), \qquad P_l(t)=F_e(t)v(t) $$
